# UTM Framework (Standard)

## UTM Rules
- utm_source: google | bing | facebook | instagram | youtube
- utm_medium: cpc | paid_social | display | video
- utm_campaign: {brand}_{service}_{geo}_{month}{year}
- utm_content: {hook}_{format}_{creativeid}
- utm_term: {keyword} (search only)

## Examples
- utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=brand_rhinoplasty_miami_feb2026&utm_content=confidence_video_v1&utm_term=rhinoplasty%20miami
